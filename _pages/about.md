I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the Stockholm School of Economics, with an MPhil from the University of Oxford. My research interests are in education, school choice, market design and applied IO. Beyond my academic work, I have been involved in the practical implementation of centralised school admissions in Sweden, working with municipalities on mechanism design, priority rules, and school supply planning. I am interested in questions where clean theory meets messy real-world institutions — in school choice and, more recently, in studying how rapid consolidation through sequential acquisitions reshapes markets.
